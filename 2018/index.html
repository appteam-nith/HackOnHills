Timeline
8th March 2019
Opening Ceremony and Pre Hackathon meet
All the registered teams are requested to be present at the campus of NIT Hamirpur, atleast a day before the commencement of the hackathon.
9th March 2019
Hacking begins
Eat! Sleep! Code! Repeat!
The following two days will be shear programming, hacking and developing. All the projects are to be designed and developed in these two days. There will be regular assessment of your work by our experts.
10th March 2019
Presentation and Judgement
The winner(s) of HackOnHills 2019 will be announced on 10th March 2019 and the prizes will be distributed.

FAQs
Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
Hack on hills is offline event and as a participant you need to be present at NIT Hamirpur campus one or two day before the event at least.
Do this hackathon has any participation fees for students?
No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over India.
What are all the restrictions on Team formations for the event?
●  Any Team can have at most 5 members
●  If a Team have participants from NIT Hamirpur or IIIT Una, then the team members are restricted to contain atmost 3 members from 3th Year, 4th Year or 5th Year.
What should I do if I do not have any team yet?
Just fill out the form leaving team name as blank, and according to skills you have filled out we will match you with the best team possible during Pre-Hackathon meeting.
What are the Perks and Benefits of Participating in this Hackathon?
Hackathon Provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the Projects like Crypto Currency, AI, Machine Learning. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshment and to keep them up.
What are the Accommodation Facility for the Participant from Outside the College?
Participant will be provided with Accommodation and Food in the Guest House of College during the Hackathon Period.
