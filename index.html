Welcome coders, hackers and developers to HackOnHills 2018!

Coding Phase
( 24th - 25th March )

Eat! Sleep! Code! Repeat!

The following two days will be shear programming, hacking and developing. All the projects are to be designed and developed in these two days. There will be regular assessment of your work by our experts.
At the conclusion of these two days, every team has to present their project before the judges.

Prize Distribution
( 25th March )

The winner(s) of HackOnHills 2018 will be announced on 25th March 2018 and the prizes will be distributed.

It is not about just winning, hackathons are where your crazy idea becomes reality!
